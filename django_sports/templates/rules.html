Rules to Site

Welcome to SportsBrokerage!

This website is meant to act like a free market for the sports betting industry. Investors can look across different futures in sports such as basketball, baseball, and football, and choose which bets they would like to make. These bets are represented as shares that you can buy a certain amount of at the given price(s), which can then either be sold at a later date or held until the outcome is decided, with the hopes that your shares pay out. Payout has been set to $10/share for the winner.

Buying and Selling Shares

Shares can be purchased from the homepage, which takes you to the different futures bets one can make. Each bet will tell you how many shares are available, and the price of each batch of shares. You input how many shares you'd like, and the highest price you'd be willing to pay for each share. If there's enough shares for your desired price, you will pay the asking price of that amount of shares (eg. 10 shares sold at $1.00/share, 10 sold at $2.00/share, you want 15 shares and would be willing to pay $3.00/share, you would end up buying all 10 shares at $1.00/share and 5 shares at $2.00/share).

Conversely, if you're ready to sell, you can go to your account page and click the share you'd like to sell. You simply input how many shares you'd be willing to sell and the price you'd like to sell at. Those shares then get added to the list of available shares on the bets page, and shares are sold on a "first posted, first sold" basis ascending from the lowest price.

Games

This site also offers regular spread / moneyline bets on the games section. Rules apply like any other sportsbook. Enter how much you wish to either risk or win on whichever bet you choose.
